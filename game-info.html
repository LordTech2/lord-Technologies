Game Info

Developers: Dylan T. Read

Release Date: 2024

Game Components

The game includes:

  • Modular game board tiles (36 tiles for endless combinations)
  • Character pieces with unique abilities (12 characters to choose from)
  • Resource cards for weapons, health, and supplies (over 100 cards)
  • Zombie miniatures representing different threats (30 unique designs)
  • Dice for combat and skill checks (custom dice for unique mechanics)
  • Scenario book with over 20 different missions and objectives
  • Player reference sheets for quick rule lookups

Gameplay Mechanics

Players will experience a mix of strategy and luck:

  • Turn-Based Play: Players take turns moving, exploring, and fighting. Each turn has a time limit to keep the game fast-paced.
  • Resource Management: Gather supplies to survive and upgrade your character. Players must make tough choices about what to carry.
  • Combat System: Engage in battles using dice rolls and character abilities. Players can choose to attack, evade, or use items during combat.
  • Event Cards: Draw cards that introduce random challenges and opportunities, such as weather changes or sudden zombie ambushes.
  • Cooperative Objectives: Complete missions that require teamwork, such as scavenging for supplies or rescuing other survivors.

Interactive Feature: Game Setup Guide

Follow the steps below to set up your game:

  1. Choose a game mode: Cooperative or Competitive.
  2. Assemble the modular board tiles according to the scenario you choose.
  3. Distribute character pieces and initial resources.
  4. Set the starting zombie spawn points based on the scenario.

Tip: Always communicate with your team about resource management and strategy!
