FAQ

General Questions

Q: How many players can play?

A: The game is suitable for 2-4 players, allowing for cooperative strategies. Larger groups can play in teams for a more immersive experience.

Q: What is the average game length?

A: Expect 60-120 minutes of gameplay, depending on player decisions and strategies. The modular board adds variability that can extend or shorten playtime.

Gameplay Questions

Q: Are there expansions planned?

A: Yes, we are working on expansions to introduce new scenarios, characters, and zombies! Keep an eye on our website for announcements.

Q: Can I play solo?

A: While the game is designed for multiple players, solo rules are being developed for those who wish to face the apocalypse alone. Solo play will feature unique challenges and scenarios.

Community and Support

Q: How can I provide feedback?

A: We welcome community feedback! Join our forums or contact us through our website. Your input helps us improve the game!

Q: Are there official tournaments?

A: Yes, we will be hosting tournaments and events! Stay tuned for announcements on our social media channels. Winners will receive exclusive prizes and recognition!
