Playbook

Rules

1. Assemble the game board by arranging the modular tiles according to the setup guide. Each configuration changes the dynamics of the game.

2. Distribute initial resources to each player, including weapons and health markers. Players can trade resources during the game.

Gameplay Mechanics

Each turn consists of a movement phase and an action phase. Players must navigate the board, engage in combat, and manage resources wisely to survive.

Combat System

Combat is resolved through dice rolls and character abilities:

  • Roll a die to determine the outcome of your attack. Higher rolls indicate successful hits.
  • Use character abilities to enhance your chances of survival. Each character has unique skills that can be leveraged strategically.
  • Defeated zombies may drop resources or trigger events. Players must decide whether to risk exploring these areas.

Winning and Losing

To win, players must survive through all levels, defeat the final boss, and complete any required objectives. If all players die or fail to complete objectives within a set number of turns, the game is lost.

Tips for Success

  • Communicate with your team to share resources and strategies. Teamwork is essential for survival.
  • Explore cautiously; some areas may hold valuable supplies but also dangerous threats. Always have an escape plan.
  • Prioritize objectives to increase your chances of survival. Completing missions can yield valuable rewards.
  • Use the environment to your advantage. Set traps and barricades to slow down zombie hordes.
